PERSOL’S OUTSTANDING FEATURES

GLASSES MATERIAL

ACETATE

Acetate from​ cotton and powdery pigments blend to form​ variations that can only be found in Persol glasses, unique for each frame. Made by hand with the highest quality materials, all these glasses are a natural work of art.

POLARIZED LENSES

PERSOL POLARIZED SUNGLASSES

All models are produced using artisan techniques, while the high-quality polarized lenses reduce eyestrain and significantly enhance the clarity of images, blocking all glare from the sun and providing full protection from harmful UV rays.

FLEXIBLE SYSTEM

MEFLECTO

Patented in the ‘30s, the Meflecto is the first flexible stem system designed for maximum comfort by eliminating pressure on the head from glasses. This system allows glasses to adapt to any face shape and still today represents a technologically unsurpassed solution.
